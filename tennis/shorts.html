Tennis Shorts

6 real picks — Budget, Best Value, and Premium. Built for lateral movement, not the gym.

Home / Tennis / Shorts

Tennis shorts need to handle explosive lateral movement without riding up, bunching, or restricting your stride. Three things matter most: 4-way stretch moves with you on wide lunges, an inner brief liner prevents riding up and holds a spare ball between points, and a 7" inseam is the court standard — enough coverage without restricting stride on big shots.
