Best Tennis Shorts 2025

6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — built for lateral movement, not the gym.

Home › Tennis › Shorts
Tennis shorts need to handle explosive lateral movement without riding up, bunching, or restricting your stride. Three things matter most: 4-way stretch (moves with you on lunges), inner liner (prevents riding up and holds a spare ball), and 7" inseam (court standard for coverage without restricting stride).
