Best Tennis Shirts & Polos 2025

6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — breathable, wicking, and ready for the court.

Home › Tennis › Shirts & Polos
A good tennis shirt wicks sweat fast, allows a full range of arm motion, and doesn't cling when you're dripping. Avoid pure cotton on court — it absorbs sweat and stays wet all match. Look for polyester or polyester blend fabrics with moisture-wicking tech. If your club requires a collar, choose a polo.
