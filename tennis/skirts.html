Best Tennis Skirts & Dresses 2025

6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — style and function, with pockets.

Home › Tennis › Skirts & Dresses
A good tennis skirt does three things: stays in place during lateral movement, has built-in compression shorts for coverage, and wicks sweat fast. Always look for a ball pocket inside the compression shorts — holding a spare ball in your hand during serve games is genuinely annoying. Most clubs require 12"+ length.
