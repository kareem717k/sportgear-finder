A good tennis skirt does three things: stays in place during explosive lateral movement, has built-in compression shorts for coverage and confidence, and wicks sweat fast so it doesn't weigh down. Always look for a ball pocket inside the compression shorts — holding a spare ball in your hand during serve games is genuinely annoying. Most clubs require 12"+ length.
