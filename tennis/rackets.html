6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — so you always know exactly what to buy.

Home › Tennis › Rackets
The racket is your most important equipment decision. Beginners need a large head (110+ sq in) and light weight. Intermediate players want a 100–105 sq in head with more control. Advanced players go smaller (95–100 sq in) and heavier. Head size, weight, and beam width are the three numbers that matter most.
