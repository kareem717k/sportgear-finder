Best Tennis Strings 2025

6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — the most underrated performance upgrade in tennis.

Home › Tennis › Strings
If your racket came pre-strung from a store, it's almost certainly strung with cheap strings at low tension. A restring can immediately transform how your racket plays. String types: Synthetic gut = soft, arm-friendly, cheap. Polyester = durable, spin-focused, firm. Natural gut = best feel, most expensive. Multifilament = softer poly alternative.
