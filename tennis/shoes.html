Best Tennis Shoes 2025

6 real picks across Budget, Best Value, and Premium — never play in running shoes again.

Home › Tennis › Shoes
Never play tennis in running shoes. Lateral movement wears through running shoes in days and puts your ankles at injury risk. Tennis shoes have reinforced outsoles, lateral support, and herringbone patterns built for court movement. Tip: go half a size up — feet swell during long sessions.
